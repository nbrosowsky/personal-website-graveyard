Welcome to the experiment!

On every trial you'll see a color word (e.g., "red", "green") in a colored font (e.g., "red", "red")

Your task is to ignore the word and identify the color of the font.

Use the keyboard to make your response:

Press "R" if the color is red, "B" if the color is blue, "G" if the color is green, or "Y" if the color is yellow. Try answer as quickly and as accurately as possible.

Press the "start" button to begin the first trial.
